MinaPadi

Discover the fundamentals of Minapadi; a combined rice farming and fish farming in one place. Participants will be introduced to rice farming from how the field is plowed until the harvesting process as well as how fish can grow in the same pond. This program is suitable for groups, families, or individuals of all ages. Contact us to discover what program is best suited for you.

What you will get from this program

Learning about rice farming from scratch to harvest fiber_manual_record
Entertaining outdoor activities fiber_manual_record
Introduction to aquaculture fiber_manual_record
Learning about the process of food production fiber_manual_record
Increased food awareness fiber_manual_record

Duration: 3 to 3.5 hours

Urban Farming

In this program, participants will learn basic techniques on how to grow their own food within urban areas. Our aim is to teach people, especially the young generation to live sustainably and nurture nature skills for later in life. Individuals or groups of all ages or school levels are welcome to apply. Contact us to discover what program is best suited for you.

What you will get from this program
