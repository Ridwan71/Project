Waste Management

Learn how to add value to your waste from our established waste management system. In this program you will learn how to sort your waste properly and create something out of it. We aim to raise environmental awareness and public participation in waste sorting by encouraging people through our informative and interactive program. Contact us to learn how to live sustainably with zero waste. Individuals or groups of all ages or school levels are welcome to apply.
